Stage 1 runs [`../sub-prompts/prompt-1-research.md`](../sub-prompts/prompt-1-research.md):
it researches every step required before H. R. 9510 v4.0 could be submitted to
Congress and confirms each process fact is current for 2026. The result is
[`output-1-research.md`](output-1-research.md).

## Contents

- [`output-1-research.md`](output-1-research.md) - the four-phase pre-introduction
  path (settle one text; secure a sponsor; professional drafting and the 2026
  requirements; the eHopper introduction), the 2026 timing cautions, and a table
  mapping each step to the deliverable that satisfies it.

## What this stage produced

- The decision to keep the **FD&C Act amendment** as the vehicle and the standalone
  bill as supporting material.
- The committee of jurisdiction: **House Energy and Commerce (Health)**.
- The pre-introduction checklist that the twelve deliverables (LaTeX appendices in
  v4.0) must satisfy.
