\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Appendix C. The Financial-Data Transparency Standard (Non-Operative)}
\usccrosshead{APPENDIX C. THE FINANCIAL-DATA TRANSPARENCY STANDARD (NON-OPERATIVE)}

\uscprov{1}{This appendix is non-operative. It generalizes what sections
515D(k) and 5 of this Act do into a reusable standard for the financial
transparency of an automated process: every cost recorded (completeness),
every figure traceable to a record (traceability), every series aligned to the
fiscal year (periodicity), and every term used in the controlled federal
budget vocabulary (comparability). The prior bill established an
explainability standard for how legislation is created; this version
establishes the matching standard for what the process costs
\cite{kawchak2026vvuq05bill, gao-greenbook}.}
