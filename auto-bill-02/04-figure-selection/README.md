produced [`output-4-figure-selection.md`](output-4-figure-selection.md): the
complete visual plan for H. R. 9510 v5.0 - eleven numbered figures plus the
cover (six ASCII, six gray-scale Mermaid, no Mermaid family repeated) and
fifteen full-width tables of financial data, with the three-media rule (table
for enumerable data, ASCII for fixed-width alignment, Mermaid for topology)
applied slot by slot, and the placement rules the bill stages must follow
(centered white-background frames, right-aligned `R` dollar columns,
illustrative-figure marking, continuous numbering).
